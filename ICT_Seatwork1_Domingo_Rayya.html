Character Description

Cheshire Cat

In Wonderland's realm, where dreams take flight,The Cheshire Cat grins, a trickster of delight. With a wily smile, it appears and fades away, Leaving a trace of mystery in its mischievous play.

Mad Hatter

In Wonderland's swirl, where whimsy unfurls,The Mad Hatter laughs, with a hat that twirls. Tea-time is chaos, with joy in his eyes, madness and mirth in Wonderland skies.

Tweedle Dum and Tweedle Dee

The playful twins who love games, Tweedle dum and Tweedle dee are there names."You can't have one without the other" they cheer, an everlasting bond, crystal clear.

The Blue Caterpillar

Blue Caterpillar, wise and slow, Hookah puffing, in Wonderland's glow. Cryptic whispers, questions so sly, Guiding Alice as time drifts by

Alice

Alice, in Wonderland's enchanting trance, A blue dress, a ribbon, a curious dance. Rabbits and cats, a whimsical sight, In Wonderland, where dreams take flight.
